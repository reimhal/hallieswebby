Why I Love Traveling

Travel blends curiosity, planning, and spontaneity. I love seeing how landscapes, food, architecture, and local traditions can make a place feel unforgettable.

Some of my favorite kinds of trips include national parks, walkable cities, coastlines, and places with strong music or cultural history.

Dream Trip Priorities

  1. Choose the kind of destination you want most.
    • Mountains and hiking trails
    • Historic cities with museums
    • Coastal towns with good food
  2. Build a flexible itinerary with room for exploring.
  3. Keep a journal of favorite views, meals, and memories.

What Makes a Great Trip

A great trip balances structure and surprise. I like to pick one anchor activity each day, then leave space to wander, take photos, and notice the details that make a place feel personal.
